### 1. Li et al. (2023) — Toward Sustainable HPC

| 属性<br>Field | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 文件<br>File | `论文/Toward Sustainable HPC - Carbon Footprint Estimation and Environmental Implications of HPC Systems.pdf` |
| 引用<br>Citation | arXiv:2306.13177, SC'23 |

> 把 HPC 碳足迹拆成制造阶段（embodied）和运行阶段（operational），在 Frontier、LUMI、Perlmutter 上用了小时级电网碳强度数据。
> Splits HPC carbon into embodied (manufacturing) and operational (runtime) parts, and applies hourly grid carbon intensity on Frontier, LUMI, and Perlmutter.

#### 计算方法
#### Calculation method

| 维度<br>Dimension | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 总公式<br>Total | `C_total = C_em + C_op` |
| Embodied | 处理器：`M_proc = (FPA + GPA + MPA) × A_die / Yield`；存储：`M_m/s = EPC × Capacity`；封装：`150g × N_IC`<br>Processors: `M_proc = (FPA + GPA + MPA) × A_die / Yield`; storage: `M_m/s = EPC × Capacity`; packaging: `150g × N_IC` |
| Operational | `C_op = I_sys × E_op`，`E_op` 含 PUE，用 carbontracker 实测<br>`C_op = I_sys × E_op`; `E_op` includes PUE; measured via carbontracker |
| 输入数据<br>Inputs | 芯片 die area、厂商 sustainability report、Electricity Maps / UK ESO API 小时级 CI、benchmark 能耗<br>Die area, vendor sustainability reports, hourly CI from Electricity Maps / UK ESO API, benchmark energy |
| 假设<br>Assumptions | PUE 跨系统取常数；fab yield = 0.875；DRAM/SSD/HDD EPC 分别为 65/6.21/1.33 gCO₂/GB<br>Fixed PUE across systems; fab yield = 0.875; DRAM/SSD/HDD EPC at 65/6.21/1.33 gCO₂/GB |
| 局限<br>Limits | PUE 没按时变；没区分 location/market-based；idle power 没单独建模<br>PUE not time-varying; no location vs market split; idle power not modeled separately |

### 2. Lannelongue et al. (2021) — Green Algorithms

| 属性<br>Field | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 文件<br>File | `论文/Green Algorithms - Quantifying the Carbon Footprint of Computation.pdf` |
| 引用<br>Citation | *Advanced Science*, 8(12), 2100707 |

> 给出了一个可以直接套用的算法碳足迹公式和网页计算器，把 CPU/GPU 功耗、运行时间、PUE 和当地电网碳强度乘在一起。
> Provides a plug-in formula and web calculator: CPU/GPU power × runtime × PUE × local grid carbon intensity.

#### 计算方法
#### Calculation method

| 维度<br>Dimension | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 能耗公式<br>Energy | `E = t × (n_c × P_c × u_c + n_m × P_m) × PUE × 0.001` [kWh] |
| 碳排放公式<br>Carbon | `C = E × CI`；合并：`C = t × (n_c×P_c×u_c + n_m×P_m) × PUE × CI × 0.001`<br>`C = E × CI`; combined form above |
| 输入数据<br>Inputs | 核心数、CPU/GPU TDP、利用率、内存功耗、数据中心 PUE、国家/地区 CI<br>Core count, CPU/GPU TDP, utilisation, memory power, datacenter PUE, country/region CI |
| 假设<br>Assumptions | 默认全球 PUE=1.67、CI=475 gCO₂e/kWh；利用率缺失时用 100%；不算 embodied<br>Defaults: PUE=1.67, CI=475 gCO₂e/kWh; 100% util if missing; no embodied term |
| 局限<br>Limits | 默认 PUE/CI 偏差大；不区分 idle/active；无 market-based<br>Default PUE/CI can be off; no idle/active split; no market-based CI |

### 5. Liu & Zhai (2025) — Carbon Emission Modeling (CEM)

| 属性<br>Field | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 文件<br>File | `论文/Carbon Emission Modeling for High-Performance Computing-Based AI in New Power Systems with Large-Scale Renewable Energy Integration.pdf` |
| 引用<br>Citation | *Processes*, 13(2), 595 |

> 针对含大量可再生能源的电力系统，建了 HPC 全生命周期碳排放模型 CEM；运营排放约占 87%，提高可再生比例能明显降碳。

#### 计算方法
#### Calculation method

| 维度<br>Dimension | 内容<br>Content |
|------|------|
| 总公式<br>Total | `C = C_em + C_op` |
| Embodied | `C_em = C_x + C_m + C_s + C_e + C_t + C_d`（芯片/内存/存储/设备/运输/部署）<br>Chip, memory, storage, equipment, transport, deployment |
| Operational | `C_op = ∫₀ᵀ Le(t,r) × E_op(t) × U_o(t) dt` |
| 能耗<br>Energy | `E_op(t) = (Σ P_srv,i + Σ P_gpu,j + Σ P_net,k) × η` |
| 输入数据<br>Inputs | 芯片/内存/存储 embodied 系数、峰值功率、PUE(η)、可再生缺口率 Le、利用率 U_o(t)<br>Embodied coeffs, peak power, PUE (η), renewable deficit Le, utilisation U_o(t) |
| 假设<br>Assumptions | 用峰值功率简化；PUE=1.5；Carbontracker 2.1.0 辅助参数；处置阶段 <0.1% 忽略<br>Peak power simplification; PUE=1.5; Carbontracker 2.1.0 for ops params; disposal <0.1% dropped |
| 局限<br>Limits | 峰值功率会高估动态负载；Le 参数化复杂；未区分 market-based<br>Peak power overstates dynamic loads; Le is hard to parameterise; no market-based CI |
